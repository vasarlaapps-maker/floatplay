FloatPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

FloatPlay ("the App") is a macOS application that displays streaming video in a floating window. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

FloatPlay does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or any data tracking.

Web Browsing

FloatPlay uses a built-in web browser (WebKit) to load streaming platforms you choose. Login credentials and cookies are stored locally on your device using the standard macOS WebKit data store and are never accessed or transmitted by the App.

Third-Party Services

When you use FloatPlay to visit streaming websites (e.g. YouTube, Netflix), those websites' own privacy policies apply. FloatPlay has no control over and assumes no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

Contact
